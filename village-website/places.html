Places to Visit in Visakhapatnam

Popular Attractions

  • R.K. Beach: A serene beach perfect for evening walks and relaxation.
  • Kailasagiri: A hilltop park with breathtaking views of the city and coastline.
  • Simhachalam Temple: A historic temple dedicated to Lord Narasimha.
  • Araku Valley: A picturesque hill station near Vizag known for coffee plantations.
